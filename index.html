TOR VANITY ADDRESSES FOR BITCOIN


The TOR network is becoming increasingly popular, particularly after big companies such as facebook decided to use it for their regular business. However, hosting a website on TOR is a tedious business, as there is no traditional DNS system available to provide easy to remember hostname addresses. We like to change that by providing a service to manage pre-generated vanity addresses.

Our vanity addresses are generated on a large scale with a sophisticated algorithm that is able to efficiently mine the huge search space of possible 16-char combinations. Furthermore our vanity addresses are much stronger than the one generated with free software such as Shallot or Scallion. This is because those are generating RSA key pairs, that include public exponent which is only an odd number, and not a prime. On the other hand, our hostnames are based on RSA keys, generated by strong prime numbers that pass the Miller-Rabin primality test and the public exponent is guaranteed to be a primer number. Thus, we generate hostnames based on non-vulnerable RSA key-pairs.

We sell the generated vanity addresses for a flat rate of 0.1 Bitcoins. Once you order the desired vanity address, we will generate the corresponding private key, and send it to you. Then the vanity address will be removed from our database. We can also prove that we own the given hostname by hosting a dummy website on the given vanity address.
